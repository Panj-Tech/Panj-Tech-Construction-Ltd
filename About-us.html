PANJ-TECH CONSTRUCTION LIMITED is a construction company trading under a registered name; PANJ-TECH CONSTRUCTION LIMITED. It is a privately owned company that was established in 2019 with the goal of taking the current lead in construction and growing it within Uganda and in the international spheres. The primary function of the company is to provide construction services to developers, funding agents and developing organizations. Our management and staff have a complimentary experience in project management, production management and site supervision.

To become the leading service provider in project management in various civil engineering disciplines based on the capabilities of the organization.
